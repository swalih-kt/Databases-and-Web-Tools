About

speciPFIC is a meticulously curated database aimed at providing a comprehensive resource for clinicians and geneticists focusing on Progressive Familial Intrahepatic Cholestasis (PFIC- I, II, and III). Leveraging extensive literature surveys, we assembled a panel of three genes implicated in PFIC, namely ATP8B1, ABCB11, and ABCB4. Through rigorous curation from ClinVar, LOVD, Google Scholar, and other data resources, we have systematically annotated genetic variants within these genes.
